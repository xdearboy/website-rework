**1. Programming**
It's not just a hobby - it's my life. I live code, automations, tasks. My future profession is software engineer. I write something new every day.

**2. Music**
I listen to it 24/7. A few years ago I even wrote my own. Chances are, right now on [dev0.cfd](https://dev0.cfd/) you can see what's currently playing.

**3. English**
I've been learning it since 2017 - 8 years now. It helps me understand documentation, watch all kinds of videos, and be part of the global IT community.

**4. Communication**
People are made to talk, share, and understand each other. Communication isn't just words - it's emotions, energy, ideas.

**5. Love**
Without it, a person is empty. Love comes in many forms - for people, for ideas, for your cat. When my cat is next to me, I smile - and the whole world feels warmer.
