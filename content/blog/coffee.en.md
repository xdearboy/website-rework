## what you need

- strawberry syrup (eyeball it, about 7% of the cup)
- cocoa powder (2 teaspoons)
- a piece of chocolate (10-15 g is enough)
- espresso (~100 ml)
- steamed milk (from the pitcher, 100-150 ml)
- (if you don't have syrup) a couple drops of water in the cocoa

## how I make it

1. take a cup and toss in the syrup, cocoa, and chocolate.
2. stir without the espresso first - you'll get a thick paste.
3. brew the espresso and pour it into that paste, stir quickly: the chocolate melts and the cocoa opens up.
4. pour in the steamed milk from the pitcher.
5. if you don't have syrup, add a couple drops of water so the cocoa mixes in better.

## variations

- instead of strawberry syrup, try caramel or hazelnut - also really good.
- swap the milk for cream for something even richer.
- a pinch of cinnamon adds a nice spice.
- like it stronger? add another shot of espresso.
